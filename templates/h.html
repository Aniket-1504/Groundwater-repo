About Project

The Real-Time Groundwater Resource Evaluation System leverages Digital Water Level Recorder (DWLR) datasets, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Time-Series Analysis to predict groundwater levels with high accuracy. The platform analyzes historical and real-time groundwater data alongside rainfall, temperature, and environmental parameters to provide forecasts, trend analysis, interactive visualizations, and early warning alerts. It empowers researchers, government agencies, and policymakers to make informed decisions for sustainable groundwater resource management.
